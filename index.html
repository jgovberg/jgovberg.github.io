The journey of two ice cream addicts

Two ice cream lovers, Julia Govberg and Eliza Reinisch, decided to commit their ice cream passion to posting on social media. Found in June of 2017, Outtaconetrol displays their any ice cream adventures. They are committed to sharing their love of ice cream with the world.

We have travelled and tasted ice cream from shops all across the world, from Philadelphia and New York City to Rome and Paris.

Little Baby's Ice Cream

Located in West Philadelphia, Little Baby's Ice Cream is a hidden treasure. They have many creative and unique homemade flavors.

Ben and Jerry's

The classic Ben and Jerry's is one of Outtaconetrol's favorite ice cream shops. They have great flavors and their passion for social justice makes the shop have a great and positive environment.

Franklin Fountain

One of our favorite places in Philadelphia is Spruce Street Harbor Park, and it is home to the great Franklin Fountain. We love their milkshakes and waffle cones!
